RED CARPET MUSEUM



Red Carpet Museum is an important tourist attraction based in Mumbai city in Maharashtra. There are wax figures and statues of great leaders, sportsmen, celebrities, politicians, scientists, innovators and many more. Everyone who visits the museum can fulfill the dream of getting close to a star by posing with stars. and having fun with friends and family at one of the best destination places in Mumbai none other than Red Carpet Museum. Get amazing experience just by standing beside stars like Michael Jackson, Barak Obama, Mr. Bean statues!
